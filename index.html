How to help

Bercerita is a non-profit that prepares future leaders with the skills to compete globally and work with people from diverse cultures and backgrounds by: Partnering with elementary schools in the rural part of the world to facilitate online English communication courses taught by native English-speaking high-school student volunteers. Channel funds to provide facilities, equipment, and technology to schools in rural areas of underdeveloped countries.
