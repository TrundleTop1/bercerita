Bercerita is a non-profit organization that prepares future leaders with the skills to compete
globally and work with people from diverse cultures and backgrounds by:
partnering with elementary schools in the rural part of the world to facilitate
online English communication courses taught by native English-speaking high-
school students volunteers. Channel funds to provide facilities, equipment, and
technology to schools in rural areas of underdeveloped countries.
